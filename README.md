## 📊 About

This interactive dashboard was independently designed and developed to visualize and explore the findings of an academic group research project on trade balance dynamics across six GCC countries (Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman) covering the period 2010–2024.

The research examines how digital innovation, financial inclusion, and sustainability influence trade balance performance across the GCC region.


**Research variables:**
- **Digital Innovation** – Internet use, mobile subscriptions, secure servers
- **Financial Inclusion** – Account ownership, domestic credit to private sector
- **Sustainability** – Composite sustainability index, ecological footprint, renewable energy
